Hot Caramel Apple Cider

Recipe

Instructions 1. Heat apple cider in pan or crock-pot. 2. Ladle into cup (serving sizes will vary) 3. Add cool whip and drizzle with caramel.

Spiced Orange Mocha Hot Chocolate

Recipe

1. In a large, heavy bottomed saucepan add the milk, dark chocolate and espresso. 2. On medium heat, combine the ingredients until the chocolate is partially melted. 3. Add the rest of the hot chocolate ingredients and bring to a boil. 4. Once it begins to boil, reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for about 3-4 minutes until the hot chocolate has thickened. 5. Take it off the heat. 6. Immediately pour in mugs and add the desired toppings and serve.

Homemade Egg Nog

Recipe

Instructions 1. Pour milk and half and half into a saucepan and warm over medium heat. 2. Meanwhile, separate egg whites and yolks. Add yolks to a medium sized mixing bowl. Add sugar. Using a whisk, beat the yolks until pale. 3. Using a paring knife, slice open the vanilla bean lengthwise and scrape out the seeds. Set aside. (nutmeg - add pod and nutmeg to milk) 4. Once the milk it hot, use a small ladle to transfer some hot milk to the bowl with the egg yolks. Immediately whisk the hot milk into the egg yolks. Repeat this process until half of the milk has been combined with the eggs. Then pour the rest of the hot milk into the egg yolks and whisk. 5. Pour the egg nog back into the saucepan and heat over medium heat to 160F. Take off the heat, add the vanilla bean, and whisk.

Blackberry Hot Chocolate

Recipe

Instructions 1. Make the blackberry coulis: cook the blackberries with some powdered sugar and a splash of water until soft, then push through a sieve to remove the seeds 2. Make the hot chocolate by heating the milk until scalding then pouring over chopped chocolate. Next, add vanilla extract and some coulis and whizz everything together in a blender until frothy 3. Garnish: I like to go to town on this decadent drink and serve it with a swirl of whipped cream, a drizzle of that punchy coulis, some mini marshmallows and a blackberry

Vegan Peppermint Hot Chocolate

Recipe

Instructions 1. Heat the almond milk in a saucepan until just simmering. 2. Finely chop the chocolate and add to the almond milk along with the peppermint extract. Whisk for 3-4 minutes until completely dissolved and frothy. Pour into mugs and enjoy immediately. 3. Top with whipped cream if desired, or a vegan alternative if you prefer, or drink plain!
